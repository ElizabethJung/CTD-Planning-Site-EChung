How to log in:
Enter your assigned username into the username box and enter your assigned password into the password box, then press login.
Don't have an assigned username or password? Try your AIS student ID number. If you're a parent or visitor, use your child's or consult the office.

Password help:


If you forget your password or want to change it, click here (hyperlink to password change or a popup shows up).

Why can't I log in?


Try using a different device, most devices should still allow you to log in, but if trouble persists, consult the office.
