Be smart. Do not post images or information unrepresentative of yourself online. Do you want others to associate you with your unthoughtful username?

Stay safe. Think before you post your personal information or experience online. Would you want the world to know your home address?

Be responsible. You are given the rights to access the internet for educational purposes.

Do not use the internet to visit inappropriate websites (such as pornographic, or racist).

Be considerate. Handle other people's and the school's IT devices with care. Don't create, upload or download viruses.

Be kind. Do not bully others. If you help or support someone bullying another person, that's also bullying! If you or someone is being bullied, tell your parents or a teacher as soon as possible.
