    Which part of the page do people look

- This page is about where people usually look at first on a webpage. Using slow motion function to track eye movement, it is found that the most looked at areas are in shape of a "F" as shown below.

Using this information, our team is going to present the important texts on these red areas. Buttons that redirect to main sites (such as for logging in) are also going to be placed on the left on the page so that it is quickly seen by viewers and their needs can be met quickly.
