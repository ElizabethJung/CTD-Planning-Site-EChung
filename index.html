AIS Wifi help site

In this project, Jet Lam, Jenny Tong, Pholopat Koo, and I , will make the AIS wifi help page more user friendly and accessable for our client Mr. Chiu.

This site is to give information on wifi troubles

    Objectives:
  • aim for middle school students
  • include information on wifi login
  • include information on wifi usage
  • code of conduct when using the AIS wifi
  • educate middle school students on how they can make good use of technology to collaborate
  • appeal to middle school students (theme)
  • for middle school students to find answers they have on wifi on this page
  • for middle school students to feel that this site is fun and entertaining whether than strict and professional
      • Success criteria:
  • Ais logo
  • Title: AIS HELP
  • Splash page
  • 3 categories (Logging in, Rules and Regulations, Tech Usage Expectations)
  • Include AIS banner "Login info" button that brings users to the corresponding section of the page
