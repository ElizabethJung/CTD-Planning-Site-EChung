AIS Wifi help site

In this project, Jet Lam, Jenny Tong, Pholopat Koo, and I , will make the AIS wifi help page (for middle school) more user friendly and accessable for our client Mr. Chiu and Mrs. Smith. Mr. Chiu is our current Computer Design teacher and Mrs. Smith is the middle school principle. This is this finished results: NEW AIS wifi help page

This site is to give information on wifi troubles, i.e logging in and technology tips.

    Objectives:
  • educate middle school students on how they can make good use of technology to collaborate
  • appeal to middle school students (theme)
  • for middle school students to find answers they have on wifi on this page
  • for middle school students to feel that this site is appealing rather than strict and professional
      • Success criteria:
  • Title and template
  • Splash page
  • 3 categories (Logging in, Rules and Regulations, Tech Usage Expectations)
  • Include AIS banner "Login info" button that brings users to the corresponding section of the page
    • Flowchart of Idea 3 (page layouts)

    Buttons:

    - I need 5 buttons for this planning site (Index, brainstorm, subpage, homepage, and learning)

    - buttons can be used for navigation and linking webpages

    - Organize my content

    - My aim is to have these button so users do not need to take that extra effort to click back space and then click the webpage

    - I have divided it this way (Index, brainstorm, subpage, homepage, and learning) because for future teachers looking at my work, it wouldn't be confusing. Having the buttons called: WP1 or WP2, wouldn't make any sense to new comers.
